      <h1 class="page__title node__title mt-0"><span class="field field--name-title field--type-string field--label-hidden">Random Testing Rates</span>
</h1>
    

    
    
          <div class="mb-4 clearfix">
        
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">2023 DOT Random Testing Rates</h2>

<table border="1" cellpadding="1" cellspacing="1" style="width: 100%; height: 100%;"><caption>
	<div>The following chart outlines the annual minimum drug and alcohol random testing rates</div>

	<div>established within DOT Agencies for 2023.<br />
	 </div>
	</caption>
	<thead><tr><th height="42" scope="col" style="width: 412px;" valign="center">
			<div align="center">DOT Agency</div>
			</th>
			<th height="42" scope="col" style="width: 180px;" valign="center">
			<div align="center"><strong>2023 Random Drug Testing Rate</strong></div>
			</th>
			<th height="42" scope="col" style="width: 180px;" valign="center">
			<div align="center"><strong>2023 Random Alcohol Testing Rate</strong></div>
			</th>
		</tr></thead><tbody><tr><td class="rtecenter" scope="row" style="width: 412px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration<br />
			[FMCSA]</p>

			<p>The random rates did not change for 2023.  Because the random rates did not change, FMCSA is not required to publish a notice in the Federal Register.  <a dir="ltr" href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2019-12-27/pdf/2019-28164.pdf" title="The rate last changed in 2020.">The rate last changed in 2020.</a></p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 162px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">50%</p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 180px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>10%</p>
			</td>
		</tr><tr><td class="rtecenter" scope="row" style="width: 412px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>Federal Aviation Administration<br />
			[FAA]</p>

			<p><a dir="ltr" href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2022-11-18/pdf/2022-25173.pdf" title="2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice">2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice</a></p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 162px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">25%</p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 180px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">10%</p>
			</td>
		</tr><tr><td class="rtecenter" rowspan="3" scope="row" style="width: 412px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>Federal Railroad Administration<br />
			[FRA]</p>

			<p><a dir="ltr" href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2022-12-20/pdf/2022-27553.pdf" title="2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice">2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice</a></p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 162px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">25% - Covered Service</p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 180px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">10% - Covered Service</p>
			</td>
		</tr><tr><td style="width: 162px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>25% - Maintenance of Way</p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 180px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>10% - Maintenance of Way</p>
			</td>
		</tr><tr><td style="width: 162px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>50% - Mechanical</p>

			<p><a dir="ltr" href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2022-02-02/pdf/2022-01985.pdf" title="(Effective 03/04/2022)">(Effective 03/04/2022)</a></p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 180px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>25% - Mechanical</p>

			<p><a dir="ltr" href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2022-02-02/pdf/2022-01985.pdf" title="(Effective 03/04/2022)">(Effective 03/04/2022)</a></p>
			</td>
		</tr><tr><td class="rtecenter" scope="row" style="width: 412px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>Federal Transit Administration<br />
			[FTA]</p>

			<p><a dir="ltr" href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2022-11-09/pdf/2022-24379.pdf" title="2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice">2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice</a></p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 162px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">50%</p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 180px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">10%</p>
			</td>
		</tr><tr><td class="rtecenter" scope="row" style="width: 412px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p>Pipeline &amp; Hazardous Materials Safety Administration<br />
			[PHMSA]</p>

			<p><a dir="ltr" href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2022-12-23/pdf/2022-27906.pdf" title="2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice">2023 Random Rate Federal Register Notice</a></p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 162px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">25%</p>
			</td>
			<td style="width: 180px; height: 56px; text-align: center; vertical-align: middle;">
			<p align="center">N/A</p>

<p><strong>NOTE:</strong> Employers (and C/TPAs) subject to more than one DOT Agency drug and alcohol testing rule may continue to combine covered employees into a single random selection pool.</p>

<p>USCG covered employees may be combined with DOT covered employees in drug testing pools even though the USCG is now part of the Department of Homeland Security.</p>
